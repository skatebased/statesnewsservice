Privacy Policy

Last updated 21 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how States News Service (“we,” “us”) handles information collected through this website. It covers the website only — not the licensed content feeds we deliver to clients, which are governed by separate agreements.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide. When you use our contact form we collect the name, organization, email address and message you submit.
  • Automatic information. Standard server logs and, if enabled, privacy-respecting analytics — such as IP address, browser type and pages visited — to operate and improve the site.

How we use information

We use the information you provide to respond to your inquiry and to follow up about our services, and we use automatic information to keep the site secure, reliable and useful. We do not sell personal information.

How information is shared

We share inquiry details with our sales agent, MEI Global, LLC, so they can respond to licensing questions on our behalf. We may use service providers (for example, hosting or email) that process information solely for us, and we may disclose information where required by law.

About our source content

The documents we compile come from public, official government and institutional sources. That content is public record; this policy concerns personal information collected through this website, not the material in our feeds.

Cookies & analytics

The site may use essential cookies and, where enabled, aggregate analytics. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Retention & security

We keep inquiry information only as long as needed to respond and maintain our records, and we use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it.

Your choices

To ask what information we hold about you, to correct it, or to have it deleted, email editor@statesnewsservice.com.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; the “last updated” date above reflects the current version.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email editor@statesnewsservice.com.
