Frequently asked

Before you reach out.

What exactly do you deliver?

Original press releases and documents from official government and public-interest sources, normalized to one consistent schema — every record sourced and timestamped.

What formats, and how is it delivered?

Clean XML or CSV, delivered by SFTP. We can push to your systems, or host a box you pull from — whichever fits your pipeline.

How much, and how often?

A daily feed — roughly 30,000 new articles a month — backed by an archive of more than 6.3 million documents.

Where does the content come from?

Around 2,500 official, public, non-paywalled sources — the executive, legislative and judicial branches, plus universities, think tanks, advocacy groups, associations and 28 governments abroad.

How do you ensure quality?

Human review plus a zero-error delivery gate on every push — nothing ships unchecked. We've delivered continuously since 1973.

How do we get started?

Send the form above or email editor@statesnewsservice.com. We'll reply within one business day and can send a sample feed so you can see the data.
